Come Adventure The California Redwoods

Welcome to the California Redwoods. We have hundreds of acres of beautiful redwoods here for your exploration. Our national park comes with amenities such as bathrooms, general stores, and picnic areas. Pets are welcome as long as they remain on leash. Our amentities are open to the public from 8am to 8pm. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us through the contact information provided below. Please call to reserve a campsite at any of our campgrounds as they are not first-come first-serve.
